Welcome to the Dropp sample payments application

This app provides:

  • a sample UI implementation of our 'Pay With Dropp' button
  • a callback page to preview payment data from a sandbox environment payment

To best utilize this guide, you should have an consumer account and a merchant account both set-up in the Sandbox environment. For instructions on how to set-up both types of accounts, refer to the guide here. You should be logged in to your consumer account on the browser extension

Showcasing Dropp Payment Methods

  • Single Payment
  • Credit Payment
  • Recurring Subscription Payment
  • Referral Payment
  • Distribution Payment
  • Refund Payment
